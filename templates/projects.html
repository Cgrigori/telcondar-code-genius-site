🚗 Formula 1: Anatomy of a Race Car

At Telcondar Code Genius LTD, we don’t just write code—we engineer understanding. One of our showcase projects dives into the intricate world of Formula 1 engineering by transforming a modern F1 car into a living knowledge graph.

This project breaks the car into its fundamental components—chassis, suspension, power unit, aerodynamics, telemetry, and beyond—and maps out how these parts interconnect using a Neo4j graph database. Each part is a node, every functional or mechanical relationship is a connection.

The result is an interactive knowledge system that can help fans, students, and engineers alike explore how each element of an F1 car contributes to its speed, control, and reliability.

  • 🧠 Explore technical relationships like Suspension → Connected To → Chassis
  • 🔍 Learn which parts are standardized by FIA regulations
  • ⚙️ Understand dependencies between electronics, hydraulics, and mechanical systems

Whether you're a racing fan or a systems thinker, this is a perfect example of how technology can bring complex machines to life—one node at a time.

This project combines powerful tools and techniques—including Neo4j for graph modeling, Python and Pandas for data processing, Flask for backend development, and extensive research to structure and refine the dataset.
